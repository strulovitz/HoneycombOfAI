- **Task parallelism vs model parallelism** — presented as Nir's invention/breakthrough. Chess analogy (helpers each analyze one piece type).

- **Queen Bee is a POWERFUL machine** — not just a coordinator. Three tiers: dual-GPU desktop, DGX Spark, VPS (RunPod etc). Queen's AI quality determines final product quality.
- **Pirate Bay / Privateer metaphor** — reputation system (colored skulls), Queen Elizabeth I and Sir Francis Drake. Our system = legal privateers, not pirates.
- **DeepSeek R1, chain of thought, Mixture of Experts, quantization** — explained for laypeople as why small models are now powerful enough.
- **NOT an agent** — explicit section differentiating from OpenClaw/agents. Our workers do NOT take over computers, do NOT make autonomous decisions.
- **Nir's $127 VPS mistake** — real story used as example of why Queen Bee role requires skill.
